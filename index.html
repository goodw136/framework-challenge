About Goodwin's Hospital

Get to Know Us

Hello, and welcome to Goodwin's Hospital! We're a hospital serving the Portland, Oregon area, that was started over 50 years ago in these very streets. We hope we can show you why we continue to be one of the best-reviewed hospitals in the entire county!

At our core, we hold three main values very close to our heart. Click on each one to know more about how we uphold these in every facet of our care!

We hold our staff at Goodwin's Hospital to nothing but the utmost standards of treatment quality. We expect that every one of our staff members is able and willing to provide the best, most comfortable care we can possibly provide our patients!

We at GH want you and your loved ones to be treated not only perfectly, but quickly as well. We hope to give you care without having to wait in long lines or wait for hours without being checked on. Instead, we want nothing more than to get you better as soon as possible!

At the end of the day, we want to make GH not just a place you leave feeling better physically, but mentally and emotionally as well. Our staff will treat you and your loved ones with the highest degree of care, comfort, and patience, and we are willing to hear any concerns you may have to these ends!

We hope you choose us for any treatment you may need!
